Blueberry Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh blueberries
  • ¼ cup stevia sweetener (such as Truvia®), or more to taste
  • ½ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 9 cups water, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, sweetener, lemon juice, and 1 cup water together in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a pitcher. Add remaining water and stir.
